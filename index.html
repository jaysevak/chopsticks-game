Game Rules

Basic Gameplay

Each player starts with 1 finger on each hand. Players take turns tapping an opponent's hand with one of their own hands. When a hand is tapped, the opponent adds the number of fingers on the tapping hand to the tapped hand.

Split Move

If you have more than one finger total across both hands, you can redistribute your fingers between your hands. You cannot split if you have only one finger total or if one hand is dead and the other has exactly one finger.

Dead Hand

When a hand reaches exactly 5 fingers, it is considered "dead" and can no longer be used. A hand with 0 fingers is still alive but cannot be used to attack.

Winning

A player wins when both of their opponent's hands are dead (have 5 fingers each).
